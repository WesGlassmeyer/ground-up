The Coach

BALI'S
LONGEST-RUNNING
BJJ SCHOOL

Made Suardana discovered Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu through a traveling practitioner who came through Canggu in 2009 and never fully left. What started as informal mat sessions on a surf club floor became Canggu BJJ — the island's most established grappling community.

Trained under multiple international instructors who have passed through Bali over the years, Made earned his brown belt through consistent competition and study. He has competed across Southeast Asia including Singapore, Kuala Lumpur, and Jakarta.

His classes blend technical fundamentals with the kind of open, welcoming energy that Canggu is known for. Beginners and purple belts train side by side here.
